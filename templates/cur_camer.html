Finger Pen

Your finger is more powerful than you think !

Gesture Category:

  • "One" -- Write
  • "Two" -- Eraser
  • "Three" -- Clear the screen
  • "Four" -- Start writing
  • "Five" -- End writing and Recognize
  • "Thumb up" -- Save current writing

Notes:

  • Use Gesture "Four" to start writing and you will see four rectangles in the canvas.
  • Make sure your whole hand are in the camera to get better gesture recognition result.
  • Only the words write in the region (Deep Blue Color) will be recognized.
  • Hold gesture "Thumb up" for a while to save current writing and keep writing new letters.
  • Once you use gesture "Five" to end writing, your writings will be recognized immediately.
